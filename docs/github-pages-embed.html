An interactive exploration of snakes across the world - their characteristics, domestication, media representation, and the ethics of snakeskin farming

About This Dashboard

This comprehensive dashboard provides insights into snake species worldwide, including:

  • US Overview: Heatmaps and analysis of snakes across the United States
  • Global View: Continental comparisons and worldwide species data
  • Domesticated Snakes: Pet snake species and their domestication history
  • Snakes in Media: How snakes are portrayed in movies, TV, and culture
  • Farming Ethics: Snakeskin farming practices and ethical considerations
